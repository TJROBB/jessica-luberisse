About Jessica

A decade of brand building, now working for you.

Jessica Luberisse is a brand and innovation marketer with more than ten years launching products and building brands inside some of the world's best-known companies — from Unilever and Anheuser-Busch to Tarte Cosmetics and The Honey Pot Company.

As an Innovation Brand Manager, she specializes in new product development and go-to-market strategy: taking ideas from concept to shelf, sharpening positioning, and building launch plans that actually move product. She now brings that big-brand expertise to founders and growing consumer brands as a freelance consultant.

Having built her own engaged audience as a lifestyle creator, Jessica understands modern brand-building from both sides — the boardroom and the feed — and helps clients show up with content that connects.

She holds an M.S. in Marketing with a concentration in Brand Management from New York University, and works in both English and French.
